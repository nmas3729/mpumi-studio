# MPUMI STUDIO — UI & BRAND CONTEXT

## Overview
MPUMI STUDIO is a modern nail studio brand focused on **nail health, precision, and calm artistry**.  
The website presents a **soft-futuristic, minimal, premium** aesthetic with an emphasis on emotion, cleanliness, and personal expression.

The experience is editorial and spa-like rather than loud or trendy.

---

## Brand Personality
- Calm
- Feminine but modern
- Premium / boutique
- Precise and intentional
- Clean, hygienic, trustworthy
- Emotion-led rather than sales-heavy

Tone of voice:
- Short, poetic headlines
- Gentle confidence
- No slang, no exaggeration
- Focus on feeling, care, and craftsmanship

---

## Visual Language

### Color Palette
- Soft gradient backgrounds (blush pink → white → pale blue)
- Accent pink for highlights, buttons, and emphasis text
- Black and dark gray for primary typography
- White / frosted cards for content sections

### Typography
- **Serif font** for main headlines (editorial, elegant)
- **Script / italic serif** for emotional emphasis words (e.g. “like you.”, “Perfection”, “calm hands.”)
- **Sans-serif** for body text, navigation, and labels

Typography contrast is intentional:
- Strong, large headlines
- Light, breathable body copy
- Emphasis words in pink and italic

---

---

## UX Intent
- Feel slow, calm, and intentional
- Encourage trust before conversion
- Guide users emotionally, not aggressively
- Booking is available but not forced

---

## Overall Impression
MPUMI STUDIO’s website should feel like:
- Walking into a quiet, beautiful studio
- Being cared for by an expert
- A blend of **art, wellness, and precision**
- More “editorial spa” than “beauty salon”

This context should guide:
- UI decisions
- Copywriting tone
- Motion and interaction softness
- Component spacing and hierarchy
